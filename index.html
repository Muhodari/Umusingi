Learn More About Us

Our Mission

    Is to redefine possibilities for people with disabilities, pioneering innovative pathways to economic independence. By fostering education, skill development,and financial empowerment.

Our solution

We're aim to transform the lives of individuals with disabilities. Our platform offers instant financial assistance, accessible through an innovative mobile app. Paired with a tailored training program, we expose them to market opportunities, fostering independence and unlocking their full potential.

Problem statement

    According to a 2022 UNPD report, there are over 446,000 people living with disabilities in Rwanda. Despite the commendable efforts aimed at promoting inclusivity, this demographic faces a significant challenge of unemployment and reliance on others. Many individuals are forced to beg for financial support, highlighting the urgent need for a comprehensive solution

Services

Main features of Our Solution

The following are the main features(servises) that our solution is based on.

Donation platform

This involves creating a mechanism through the online platform and mobile application to facilitate direct contributions to the bank or mobile money accounts of individuals with disabilities.

Training to people with disabilities

Beyond merely providing training, the initiative emphasizes a structured training program to people living with disabilities.

Provide skills for sustainable market

The project extends its services to ensure its own sustainability by retaining a small percentage of each donation to cover operational costs.

Community Engagement

This feature connects individuals with disabilities to a supportive network, ensuring emotional and social assistance alongside our training and skill development programs.
